Correct display of demo scene

Before opening the demo scene, increase Maximum Shadows to 300.
-Edit > ProjectSettings > Quality > HDRP > Lighting > Shadows > Maximum Shadows on Screen

---------------------------------------------------------------

Please set the occlusion culling to display the demo scene correctly.

-Bake OcclusionCulling
--Windows>Rendering>OcclusionCulling

-Camera setting
--Scene View Camera > OcclusionCulling  (the camera icon in the upper right corner of the viewport)
--Main Camera > OcclusionCulling
